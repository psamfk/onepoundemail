## The Features That Actually Matter

Let's be honest — when you're paying this little, you half expect the features list to be three items long. It isn't.

**Every plan gets:**

- **Unlimited domains** — run 1 domain or 50, same price
- **Unlimited email accounts** — no per-mailbox charges, ever
- **Catch-all email** — anything sent to @yourdomain.com gets delivered, even if the address doesn't exist. Fantastic for catching typos, one-off contact forms, or using unique addresses per service
- **Two webmail clients** — Crossbox (modern, mobile-friendly) and Roundcube (reliable, feature-rich)
- **Custom control panel** — built on DirectAdmin with an OPE-specific front-end that strips out most of the complexity. A new improved panel was released in December 2025 specifically to reduce clutter
- **IMAPSync migration tool** — move your existing emails from another host without manually re-downloading everything
- **400 outbound emails per hour** — fine for the vast majority of personal and small business use cases
- **Spam filtering** — handled at the infrastructure level before it reaches your inbox

The one thing that *isn't* included on lower plans: the 5GB Starter plan uses a slightly different control panel setup compared to the higher tiers (which use the full DirectAdmin panel). For most day-to-day use, you won't notice the difference.

## Setup: How It Actually Works

The process is fairly straightforward:

1. Sign up and pick your plan
2. Add your domain(s) — as many as you like
3. Update your domain's DNS records (MX, SPF at minimum — the docs walk you through it step by step)
4. Create your email accounts through the control panel
5. Log in via Crossbox or Roundcube webmail, or configure your email client of choice with the IMAP/SMTP settings

If you're migrating from another provider, the built-in IMAPSync tool handles the heavy lifting of pulling over your existing emails.
